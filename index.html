💡 Trip tips
Mobile ordering — order food on the MDE app 30 min before you're hungry · skip the QS line entirely
Free ice water — ask at any counter · don't buy bottled water
Rain plan — June afternoon storms are normal (30–60 min) · duck into indoor rides or restaurants · they pass quickly
Refillable mugs — ~$22 at the hotel · unlimited drinks at All-Star food court all trip · fill up before the bus each morning
Rider Switch with baby — free program at every height-requirement ride · whole group approaches entrance → Cast Member scans waiting group → first group rides via LL (~10 min) → hand off baby → second group enters via LL (~10 min) · budget ~30 min per ride · all riders need LL purchased
Times are flexible — schedule times are targets, not rigid · adjust based on actual wait times in the app
